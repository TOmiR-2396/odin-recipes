Recipe of delicious Spaguetti

Description

To make spaguetti, start by bringing a pot of salted water to a boil. Meanwhile, on a separate pan, heat olive oil and add tomato sauce, oregano and some salt. Then, drain the cooked spaguetti and add it to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly. Serve hot with grated parmesan cheese for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaguetti of your choice
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil

Step by step

  1. Boil water: Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook spaguetti: Add the spaguettis to the boiling water and cook them (follow package instructions).
  3. Prepare sauce: In a separate pan, heat olive oil and add tomato sauce, oregano and salt. Then, simmer until flavors meld.
  4. Combine: Drain the cooked spaguetti and add it to the sauce you made. Toss the coad evenly
  5. Serve: Serve hot with grated parmesan cheese and fresh herbs, if desired.
